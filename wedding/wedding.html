CEREMONY

ON THE BEACH
  1pm 12th April 2017
  Paternoster
 
Directions
Roné will scout out a location when she is in SA end of April this year and then I'll put the details up onto the website.
 
Format
Relaxed and informal. We're not fussed on enforcing a strict dress code, smart casual problie best describes what we envision. Whatever you're comfortable in. The beach may be quite hot so you might want to keep it light (barefoot or sandals or flip flops/ jandals are fine!), but it will cool down later in the evening for the reception when you can dress up a bit more if you would like. If the weather is good we'll bring picnic blankets and drinks (non-alcoholic) down to the beach so we can chill there for a bit after the ceremony. Please be aware that it might be quite hot on the beach so put sunscreen on. We'll see if we can get some umbrellas as well. Or it might be quite chilly if it's windy! So then you'll need something cozy to wrap around the arms.
 
Bad Weather Plan
If the weather is bad then we will have the ceremony at Gelukkie, where the reception is being held (see below). We will keep an eye on the weather forecast and most probably make the decision of where the day before. As mentioned on the Itinerary page, my plan is to create Whatsapp and Facebook groups to keep everyone updated on what is happening.
